Voice and Tone

Voice and tone are often used interchangeably, but they are different things. Use the guides below to learn more about the Learning Team's voice and tone.

 

Voice Elements

Our voice is who we are as a team and expresses our personality. It's the constant across all communications and everything we create.
Respectful

- We respect others time and values.

- We acknowledge and utilize others
knowledge and strengths.
Empowering

- We motivate others to constantly
grow and inspire others.

- We are confident in our skills and abilities to help
others learn and develop.
Effective

- We make learning content relevant and applicable allowing learners to use the skills immediately.

- We are intrigued by challenges and always
willing to learn.
Engaging

- We provide an engaging, positive and safe
learning-centered environment.

- We incorporate all types of learning styles
into our designs.

Tone Elements

Our tone is adaptable and flexible. Think of voice as the umbrella over tone. While voice is always consistent, tone changes depending with the learning format.

Learning Formats

Our learning format varies depending on the training needs. You can read more about the different formats our team offers.
Job Aids

Used to help learners quickly access simple, straight-forward information they need to perform on-the-job tasks.
eLearning

Used for more complicated tasks and allows the learner to "learn by doing"
before being in a real situation.
ILT

Used for highly collaborative topics and allows the learner to have discussions in a safe and positive environment.
Video

Used to communicate information at the point of need and at the specific location where that information is needed.
